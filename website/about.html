About Urbana Home Repair

Our mission is to make it possible for home improvement projects to be successful and enjoyable for everyone through strong customer relationships and high quality products.

Our business is a home improvement center that has everything our patrons need for their next project. In downtown Urbana, we will have a showroom with samples of all of the products we offer.
